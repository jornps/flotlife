Physical separation removes what floats or settles, but dissolved organic material needs
a different approach. Biological treatment uses microorganisms to break that load down,
typically as a stage after DAF, bringing effluent quality up to the standard your permit
requires. We size and configure this stage to your actual influent characteristics rather
than a one-size-fits-all design.
