## Space is the constraint. Reliability is the requirement.

Onboard a vessel or on an offshore platform, there is no room for oversized equipment — and no tolerance for systems that need constant attention. Treatment has to fit the footprint, run in motion and weather, and not create new problems for the crew.

That's the operating environment we design for.

## Where our systems fit

- **Ships and offshore installations** — treatment of process, ballast and wash water within a tight equipment footprint.
- **Ports and terminals** — reception and treatment of water discharged from visiting vessels.
- **Well boats** — treating water taken on during loading, transport and delousing operations, at the intersection of maritime and aquaculture.

## The same technology — in a maritime format

Our containerized configurations bring DAF and dewatering technology into a footprint that fits a vessel deck or platform module, with automation designed for unattended operation between crew checks. Built to run, not to be managed.

## When reliability can't be optional

A treatment system that fails at sea is a different problem from one that fails onshore. Our systems are designed for predictability: automated, remote-monitored, and built without unnecessary complexity.
