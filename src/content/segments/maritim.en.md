## Space is the constraint, reliability is the requirement

Onboard a vessel, on an offshore platform, or at a port terminal, there's no room for
oversized equipment and no tolerance for systems that need constant attention. Maritime
water treatment has to be compact, automated, and built to keep working in motion and in
weather.

## Where our systems fit at sea and in port

- **Ships and offshore installations** — treatment of process, ballast and wash water
  within a tight equipment footprint.
- **Ports and terminals** — reception and treatment of water discharged from vessels
  calling at the port.
- **Well boat operations** — the maritime side of the aquaculture value chain, treating
  water taken up during loading, transport, and delousing.

## Built for conditions on the water

Our containerized configurations bring the same DAF and dewatering technology used
onshore into a footprint that fits a vessel deck or a platform module, with automation
designed for unattended operation between crew checks.
