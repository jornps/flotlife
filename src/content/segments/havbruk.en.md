## The water your fish live in

Fish don't tolerate marginal water quality. Suspended solids, excess organics and accumulated fines eat into oxygen levels, raise disease risk, and reduce feed conversion — long before a discharge permit comes under pressure.

FlotLife Nordic removes the problem at the source: early-stage capture of feces and biosolids, before they disperse and become harder to manage.

## From waste stream to collected resource

Dissolved air flotation pulls suspended solids and fats out of the water column within minutes. What it captures doesn't have to become a disposal bill: our sludge dewatering systems reduce that material by up to 7x in volume, producing a dry, transportable cake that can go to biogas or fertilizer instead of a treatment lagoon.

[Read more about sludge as a resource →](/en/sustainability/)

## Built for every format in Nordic aquaculture

- **RAS and semi-closed systems** — continuous, automated treatment that keeps up with intensive stocking densities.
- **Sea-based sites** — compact, containerized units suited to limited deck space on rafts and barges.
- **Well boats** — onboard treatment for water taken on during loading, transport and delousing.
- **Holding pens (ventemerd)** — managing discharge at the most sensitive point before slaughter.

## Getting ahead of the regulatory curve

Sludge collection requirements for sea-based sites are tightening, particularly near sensitive seabed habitats. Facilities already running collection and dewatering won't be caught off guard when the requirements formalize.
