## Water is your product's most demanding input

Whether you run a land-based RAS facility, a sea-based site, a well boat fleet, or holding
pens ahead of slaughter, water quality is not a side issue — it's the thing your fish live
in. FlotLife Nordic builds water and sludge treatment around that reality: early capture
of feces and biosolids, stable water quality, and less sludge to deal with downstream —
not a generic industrial template.

## From discharge problem to collected resource

Dissolved air flotation removes suspended solids and fats before water is returned to the
environment or recirculated. What it captures doesn't have to become a disposal cost: our
sludge dewatering systems concentrate that material into a manageable, transportable output,
cutting handling volume and opening the door to reuse as a nutrient resource rather than
waste. Read more on our [sustainability page](/en/sustainability/).

## Built for the way Nordic aquaculture actually operates

- **RAS and semi-closed systems** — continuous, automated treatment that keeps pace with
  intensive stocking densities.
- **Sea-based sites** — compact, containerized units suited to limited space on rafts and
  barges.
- **Well boats** — onboard treatment for water taken up during loading, transport and
  delousing operations.
- **Holding pens (ventemerd)** — managing discharge in the sensitive final stage before
  slaughter, where regulatory scrutiny is highest.

## A regulatory trend you can get ahead of

Sludge collection requirements for sea-based sites are moving from voluntary to expected,
particularly near vulnerable seabed habitats. Facilities that already have collection and
dewatering in place aren't caught off guard when requirements catch up with them.
